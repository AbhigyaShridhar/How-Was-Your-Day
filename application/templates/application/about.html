Information about our Project

  • During this COVID-19 period, you may have noticed that there are lot of issues people are facing. One of the most important issue that affects each and every person is the Mental Health Care Issue. So people need to talk to each other to share their problems/ issues. So here comes up our Project Idea This website will allow users to chat anonymously with the people and share their problems and this will help them to Reduce Anxiety
  • Also, users can Upload or Record their Audio and Get Insights about the Audio that What is the Tone of the Audio? Is it Polite or Rude. What is the Frequency of words used in the audio? etc and many more.
  • Well, what if I say that Hey, can the users Connect with the people talking about the same things? The answer is YES, we have implemented this functionality where people can connect with other people having conversation about same things.
  • Last but not the least, We have also implemented a feature where Users can Directly Chat using Chat Box with the Like-minded people and have a conversation with them.
